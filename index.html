Automated SMS safety notifications for family members and caregivers

What is Fall Alert?

Fall Alert sends automated SMS text message notifications to designated family members and caregivers when safety-related events are detected, such as:

  • Fall detection
  • Prolonged inactivity
  • Room exit or absence alerts

Fall Alert is a personal, family-use safety system. It is not a substitute for professional medical monitoring or emergency services. In an emergency, always call 911.
