# Privacy Policy – Fall Alert Notification System

Last updated: March 2026

## Overview
Fall Alert is a personal safety monitoring application that sends SMS text message alerts to designated family members and authorized caregivers when certain safety-related events occur, such as fall detection or unusual inactivity.

## Information We Collect
The system stores only the mobile phone numbers of authorized recipients who have explicitly opted in to receive SMS alerts. No health data, location data, or personal identifiers beyond phone numbers are collected or shared.

## How Information Is Used
Phone numbers are used solely for delivering SMS text message alerts related to safety monitoring. Your information is never sold, shared, or used for any marketing purpose.

## SMS Messaging
- You will only receive SMS alerts after explicitly opting in through the Fall Alert app (Settings > Notifications > Enable SMS Alerts).
- Upon completing opt-in, you will receive a confirmation SMS before any safety alerts are sent.
- Message frequency varies based on detected safety events.
- Message and data rates may apply.
- To opt out at any time, reply **STOP** to any message.
- To request help, reply **HELP**.

## Data Sharing
No personal information is shared with third parties. SMS messages are transmitted solely for delivery purposes.

## Data Security
Reasonable technical measures are used to protect stored phone numbers from unauthorized access.

## Your Choices
- **Opt out:** Reply STOP to any message to immediately unsubscribe.
- **Get help:** Reply HELP to any message for assistance.
- **Remove your data:** Contact us to request deletion of your phone number.
