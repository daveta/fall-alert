Privacy Policy – Fall Alert Notification System

This application sends SMS alerts to designated family members and authorized caregivers when certain safety‑related events occur, such as fall detection or unusual inactivity.

Information We Collect  
The system stores only the phone numbers of authorized recipients. No health data, location data, or personal identifiers are collected or shared.

How Information Is Used  
Phone numbers are used solely for delivering SMS alerts related to safety monitoring. Information is not sold, shared, or used for marketing.

Data Sharing  
No information is shared with third parties. SMS messages are transmitted through Twilio for delivery.

Data Security  
Reasonable technical measures are used to protect stored phone numbers.

Your Choices  
Recipients may opt out at any time by replying STOP. They may request assistance by replying HELP.

Contact  
For questions or support, contact the system administrator.
