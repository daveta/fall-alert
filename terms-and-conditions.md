# Terms & Conditions – Fall Alert Notification System

Last updated: March 2026

## Program Description
Fall Alert is a personal safety monitoring application that sends automated SMS text message alerts (e.g., fall detection, prolonged inactivity, room exit notifications) to authorized family members and caregivers. Messages are safety-related and are sent only when a relevant event is detected.

## How to Opt In
Users opt in by:
1. Opening the Fall Alert application
2. Navigating to **Settings > Notifications**
3. Entering their mobile phone number
4. Tapping **"Enable SMS Alerts"**

By completing these steps, users provide explicit consent to receive automated SMS safety notifications on behalf of authorized caregivers. Upon completing opt-in, users will receive a confirmation SMS before any safety alerts are sent.

## Message Frequency
Message frequency varies based on detected safety events. You may receive multiple messages per day if safety events are detected.

## Cost
Message and data rates may apply. Contact your mobile carrier for details.

## How to Opt Out
Reply **STOP** to any message at any time to immediately unsubscribe. You will receive one final confirmation message and no further alerts will be sent.

## How to Get Help
Reply **HELP** to any message for assistance, or contact us directly at dave@taniguchi.com.

## Supported Carriers
This service is available on all major US carriers. Carrier support may vary.

## Limitation of Liability
Fall Alert is a personal, family-use safety notification system. It is not a substitute for professional medical monitoring or emergency services. In the event of an emergency, always call 911.
